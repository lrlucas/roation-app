# PLAYING AN AFFLICTION WARLOCK IN MIDNIGHT SEASON 1

The Affliction Warlock rotation is often perceived as overly complicated and filled with DoT maintenance. While the latter responsibility is certainly part of the spec's core gameplay, the actual rotation is quite simple and easy for players looking to play a DoT-based spec. 

Affliction Warlock is a fairly simple generator ➜ spender rotation at its core. With a few short cooldowns, there are just a small handful of rules to follow in general:

* **Do not overcap on Soul Shards:** Soul Shards are your resource and are limited to a maximum of 5. Overcapping them is a DPS loss, and they should not be pooled unless heading into a damage window.
* **Damage Windows:** You will want to find a balance between pooling Soul Shards for your Summon Darkglare window every two minutes/Malevolence every one minute (If Hellcaller), while also trying to cast and maintain Unstable Affliction effects. Spells such as Dark Harvest (if Soul Harvester) will grant additional shards to you and have a CDR based on spending shards.
* **React to procs:** Shard Instability will give you a free, instant-cast Unstable Affliction, so it's important to watch its stack count and not overcap. Both Shard Instability and Nightfall will proc often, which players need to be aware of to maximize damage and not overcap on the effects.

## BEST AFFLICTION WARLOCK SINGLE TARGET ROTATION

Affliction in Single Target plays around a priority list, our priority is more fluid than rigid and can be adapted based on what you have available to you in both resources and abilities.

With Soul Harvester selected, the priority is as follows:

**PRIORITY**
1. Cast Haunt.
2. Cast Agony.
3. Cast Corruption.
4. Cast Summon Darkglare.
5. Cast Unstable Affliction.
6. Cast Dark Harvest.

8. Cast Malefic Grasp.

## BEST AFFLICTION WARLOCK AOE ROTATION

Afflictions AOE revolves around casting as many Seed of Corruption as possible, while maintaining a few Agony and casting Dark Harvest on CD.

With Soul Harvester selected, the priority is as follows:

**PRIORITY**
1. Cast Seed of Corruption.
2. Cast Haunt on your priority target.
3. Cast Agony on a handful of targets.
4. Cast Summon Darkglare.
5. Cast Dark Harvest.
6. Cast Seed of Corruption as many times as possible.
7. Cast Malefic Grasp.

## BEST AFFLICTION WARLOCK OPENER

**OPENER SEQUENCE**

## AFFLICTION WARLOCK MAJOR COOLDOWNS

* **Summon Darkglare** is your major cooldown on a two-minute CD. It increases the damage of your active DOT effects by 20% while active, which means that you will want to cast Darkglare BEFORE you begin to chain cast Unstable Affliction. This will lead to optimal damage output by having Darkglare's damage amp cover the entire duration of your Unstable Affliction effects.
* **Dark Harvest** is a spec tree talent, but functions differently based on whether you choose Soul Harvester or Hellcaller. The ability also has cooldown reduction via Cull the Weak, making it roughly a 40-second cooldown. For Hellcaller, it is simply an effect that you will hit when it's off cooldown and you can gain damage value from it, with not much else to it. For Soul Harvester, however, it is the main capstone of the Hero Talent Tree in Shadow of Death. This effect grants you 3 Soul Shards every time you cast Dark Harvest, vastly increasing Affliction's Shard generation and overall Quality of Life. This also gives you a tad bit more CDR via Cull the Weak for Soul Harvester over Hellcaller, due to the increased Shards per minute it grants.
* **Malevolence** is your Hellcaller capstone ability and has a 1-minute cooldown. Due to the nature of Malevolence and the new Through the Felvine Hero Talent, you will want to pair this with every Darkglare cast. This means that you can cast Malevolence every minute, and have it pair back with Darkglare every two minutes. The effect itself is quite strong, granting the player a small Haste buff, while increasing the stack count of EACH active Wither by 6, and also causing targets affected by Wither to take damage.

## AFFLICTION WARLOCK ADVANCED TIPS

**GAMEPLAY AND PANDEMIC**

Affliction is not very complicated in Midnight, even with Unstable Affliction returning as your spender. You will want to cast Dark Harvest essentially on CD, as long as you are not going to overcap on Soul Shards. Keep in mind that Dark Harvest only generates Soul Shards as Soul Harvester, not Hellcaller.

Outside of that, you will want to maintain Unstable Affliction while also passively pooling shards for your Summon Darkglare window. You will also want to sync your Dark Harvest to your Darkglare window, to generate Shards (as Soul Harvester) once your pre-pooled shards have been spent.

While it is not a massive DPS loss to refresh before your Pandemic window, it is still something to min/max. Refreshing DOTs on the move however is a DPS gain (over casting nothing) and is one of the main perks of Affliction, it's incredibly mobile.

**DOT Pandemic Range**
* Agony - 4.5 Seconds
* Corruption - 4.2 Seconds
* Unstable Affliction - 6.3 Seconds
